The Documentary Club provides culture & a place for interested parties to learn about different world & cultures through the love of documented films. The Documentary Club was found in 2017 by Kells Barnett & has since grown to 100 members.

"I just knew that it had to be other open minded people out in the world who loved documentaries as much as i do, so i started the club because i love to learn about new things" says Barnett. In the future The Documentary Club aims to host events in every major city taking the same blueprint as book clubs.

Join The Documentary Club as we share out favorite documentaries with others. To submit a documentary e-mail us at TheDocumentaryClub@gmail.com
